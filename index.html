SecOps Garage Threat Suite

Your comprehensive Attack Surface Management and Threat Intelligence Platform.

Overview

Welcome to the official page for SecOps Garage Threat Suite! This platform is designed to track threat actors, analyze their behaviors, enumerate threat information, and deliver actionable intelligence. It empowers governments, law enforcement agencies (LEAs), Computer Emergency Response Teams (CERTs), Managed Security Service Providers (MSSPs), and organizational customers to effectively mitigate cyber threats.

By combining advanced threat monitoring, hacker's mindset methodologies, and curated intelligence, our platform enables proactive defense against cyber threats, protecting digital assets and safeguarding reputation.

Core Capabilities & Features

  • Nationwide Threat Landscape Monitoring: Detect infected devices, exposed credentials, vulnerable services/ports, suspicious C2 communications, and botnet activities across national infrastructures.
  • Nationwide Cyber Attack Monitoring: Identify web shells, detect illegal gambling/betting sites, monitor data breaches, track ransomware events, and alert on DDoS/defacement incidents.
  • Cyber Crime Investigation & Threat Actor Profiling: Conduct dark web investigations, monitor underground forums and Telegram channels, analyze crypto wallets, and profile nation-state and cybercriminal groups.
  • Extended Attack Surface Management (for MSSP/Organizations): Continuously scan for exposed ports/protocols/services, discover asset vulnerabilities, identify compromised devices/endpoints, and alert on compromised user credentials.
  • Digital Risk & Brand Protection: Monitor for data leaks, detect malware and phishing threats, identify impersonating/fake domains, detect rogue mobile applications, and check infrastructure reputation.
  • Curated Cyber Threat Intelligence: Provide actionable insights from collected data types including global data breaches, SOCKS/TOR proxy lists, deep/dark web intelligence, hacking forum discussions, and IOC/malware intelligence.
