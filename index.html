Project Overview

SGThreatSuite is an independent initiative that aims to uncover hidden threats, analyze and attribute threat actors, and produce **actionable intelligence for public benefit**. The platform utilizes **AI, ML, and automation** to ensure the intelligence is contextualized, accurate, and explainable. It is designed to serve a wide range of users, including **mass users, CERTs, law enforcement agencies, governments, and other organizations**, by offering breach/compromise checks, enriched threat data, and integration with existing security tools.

The project welcomes **partnerships, contributions, and direct collaboration** to strengthen national and global cybersecurity resilience.

Our Research and Project Aims

Unveiling Cyber Threat Insights: What's in it for you?

SGThreatSuite provides a range of valuable insights for users:

  • **Stolen Information** from your compromised devices.
  • **Knowledge** on your hidden attack surface.
  • **Information** on your uncovered Git Paste leaks.
  • **Insights** from Deep/Dark Web and forum exposures.
  • **Information** on data leaks and breach information if it impacts you.
  • **Cyber attack and incident information** that affects you.
